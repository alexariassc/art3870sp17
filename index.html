Alex Arias


HELLO! My name is Alex Arias and I want to thank you for taking the time in reading this, exploring my site, and just for being an overall awesome person that you are. Originally I’m from Southern California, Los Angeles, but currently I live in the Bay Area (Yay Area). I started working at a pretty young age in my fathers business and I learned something important in all that time. You have to work hard for the things you want to achieve, but that doesn’t mean you can’t have fun in throughout the journey. Being a positive and enthusiastic photographer has allowed me to meet many inspiriting people to work with and it has became a way for me to give back to the community. I’m that photographer that will always think about the benefits of others. Relationships is something that I strongly value because I find it important to have a strong connection with friends and family. Oh, and I appreciate chicken wings too (can’t go wrong with that)! That being said, hope you have a great day!
